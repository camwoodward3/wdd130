Franchise

Here are the main characters in the Super Mario Franchise.

Mario

Original Appearance: Donkey Kong (1981)

The main hero of the mushroom kingdom

Luigi

Original Appearance: Mario Bros (1983)

The kind, but coward brother of Mario, and a fellow hero to the mushroom kingdom

Peach

Original Appearance: Super Mario Bros (1985)

The beloved Princess of the Mushroom Kingdom

Toad

Original Appearance: Super Mario Bros (1985)

A very cheerful and loyal resident of the Mushroom Kingdom who has been on many adventures with Mario and Luigi

Toadette

Original Appearance: Mario Kart Double Dash (2003)

The unique pink female Toad who has been on a few adventures with Mario

Bowser

Original Appearance: Super Mario Bros (1985)

The King of the Koopas who possesses incredible strength and can breathe fire. He's the main villain of the game who always kidnapps Peach.

Yoshi

Original Appearance: Super Mario World (1990)

Mario's dependable companion who hails from Yoshi's Island and uses his long tongue to gobble up fruit and enemies.

Daisy

Original Appearance: Super Mario Land (1989)

The cheerful and energetic princess of Sarasaland

Wario

Original Appearance: Super Mario Land 2: 6 Golden Coins (1992)

The self-professed archrival of Mario who wears purple overalls and a yellow cap and loves garlic and making money

Waluigi

Original Appearance: Maro Tennis 64 (2000)

Wario's pal and accomplice and self-proclaimed rival of Luigi

Rosalina

Original Appearance: Super Mario Galaxy (2007)

The watcher of the stars and the adoptive mother of the Lumas who has been on a few adventures with Mario

Bowser.Jr

Original Appearance: Super Mario Sunshine (2002)

The only son of Bowser, the King of the Koopas
